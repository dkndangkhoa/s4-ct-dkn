Our class was recently introduced to the innovative Fietskar project by De Reuringdienst, a modular and mobile frame designed to bring social issues directly to the public. This exciting initiative challenges us to think beyond traditional approaches and engage audiences in a fun and interactive way.
The Fietskar's versatility lies in its ability to be customized with various modules. This allows us to tailor the experience to different social goals, providing informative and engaging experiences that spark conversation and raise awareness.
As part of this project, we'll be tasked with designing, building, and testing interactive modules that address a chosen social issue. This hands-on experience will not only allow us to hone our creative and technical skills but also contribute to a meaningful project that has the potential to make a real difference in our communities.
